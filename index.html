Hi! I am an educator and researcher. I'm currently a Senior Assistant Director at the Center for Advancing Teaching & Learning Through Research at Northeastern University. In my role, I work on assessment, the Scholarship of Teaching & Learning, and graduate student & postdoc development. Previously, I studied child language processing at Northwestern University. Overall, I get excited about phonetics, teaching, language, data viz, and a lot of other things too.

I am also an avid R user and educator. I'm part of the R-Ladies Global Team (see my talk about @WeAreRLadies) and the Instructor Development Committee for The Carpentries. Additionally, I love teaching R and talking to data scientists about teaching (see one of my talks here).
